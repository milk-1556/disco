Questions

The honest answers.

Is this against Discord's ToS? +

No. We build the structure of a server — channels, categories, roles and permissions — using a bot you authorize, the same way any community manager sets up a guild. We don't scrape members, copy another creator's private server without permission, or automate user accounts. You're responsible for running a community that follows Discord's Terms and Community Guidelines, including the rules around gambling and paid content; we'll flag anything in your scope that looks risky.

Do I own the server after handover? +

Completely. The build happens in your guild, and at handover we walk you through transferring full server ownership to your own account. Disco and our bot can be removed entirely — nothing keeps you locked in. The community is yours, including if you cancel monthly management later.

How long does a build take? +

Most builds are delivered within days of the scoping call, not weeks. The actual build into Discord runs in minutes — the time is in agreeing the brand, structure and any custom work up front. Bigger, fully-custom networks take longer; we'll give you a date on the call.

What transfers automatically, and what's manual? +

Channels, categories, roles, permission overwrites, emojis, stickers, AutoMod and welcome screens rebuild automatically. What Discord's API genuinely can't clone — third-party bot configs, existing member data, boost perks, and interactive panels — is handed to you as a labeled manual step with a reason. We never fake an endpoint or imply a capability that doesn't exist.

Do you need my Discord login or token? +

Never your login. We use a bot that you invite to your server and authorize, and a pre-flight check confirms it has the permissions needed before any live run. You stay in control of access the whole time and can revoke the bot the moment we're done.

What's the difference between a build and management? +

A build is the one-time work of standing up your community, clean and branded. Monthly management is optional and ongoing — we keep the server updated, help with moderation setup, structure VIP and affiliate tiers as sponsors come in, and set up add-on bots as you grow. Take the build alone, or keep us on to run it. Management can be cancelled anytime.
